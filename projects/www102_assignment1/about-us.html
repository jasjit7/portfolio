About US :

+G Visas Consultancy services was established in Canada in 2012 with the vision of our founder and Managing Director "Gurpreet Singh Dhami" to provide best, genuine and cost efficient services for immigrants from all over the world.

Our team of certified immigration lawyers provide genuine and legitimate guidance for preparing visa applications.

We provide a whole range of services as below :

  • Applying for Permanent Residency Visas, Study Visas, Work Permits, Visitor Visas
  • Pre and Post landing Services( Seminars on Canadian Lifestyle, Resume preparation, Job Search, Accommodation etc)
  • Flight booking and Airport Pickup arrangements

  • It proves our credibility for genuine evaluation.
